What I Build — And What I Don’t

I start with high-quality guitar kits and elevate them through precision setup focused on feel, playability, and style.

What I Build

  • Leveled, crowned & polished frets
  • Smoothed fret ends
  • Truss rod adjustment, action & intonation
  • Clean wiring & cavity shielding
  • Hardware upgrades where needed
  • Tested for playability before listing

What I Don’t Claim

  • Hand-carved necks or bodies
  • Boutique luthier shop claims
  • Vintage mystique or pretense
